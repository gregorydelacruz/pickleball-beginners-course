## 📖 About This Course

**Pickleball for Beginners** is a self-paced online course built for anyone who has never picked up a paddle — or has only played a handful of times and wants to build a real foundation. No athletic background required. No prior pickleball knowledge assumed.

By the end of this course, students will understand the rules, know how to move on the court, develop core skills for recreational play, and feel confident enough to show up at open play and have a great time.

The course is hosted on **Lovable** and delivered through **[beginnerscourse.bigpickleballs.com](https://beginnerscourse.bigpickleballs.com)**.

---

## 🎯 Who This Course Is For

- Complete beginners who have never played pickleball
- Casual players who want to understand the game properly
- Tennis, badminton, or ping-pong players making the switch
- Anyone who wants to play with family, friends, or at a retirement community
- Coaches and PE teachers looking for a structured curriculum resource

**No experience necessary. No equipment required to start.**
